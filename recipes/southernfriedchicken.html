Southern Fried Chicken

Description

This recipe aims to make the best Southern Fried Chicken in a satisfying manner.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onion powder
  • Cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Combine the flour and seasonings in a zip-top bag.
  2. Add the chicken to the bag and toss to coat.
  3. Chill the floured chicken in the fridge.
  4. Toss the chilled, floured chicken in the flour mixture one more time.
  5. Fry the chicken until an instant read thermometer reads 165 degreesF.
